Story Summary

Elsa and Anna

Elsa and Anna are young princesses in Aerindelle. Elsa has ice powers, and they secretly play until Elsa accidentally hurts Anna.

Trolls heal Anna

Their parents take Anna to magical trolls. The trolls heal her and erase her memory of Elsa's powers. Elsa is told to hide her magic.

Isolation and Fear

Elsa isolates herself to avoid hurting Anna again. The castle gates are closed, and the sisters grow distant over the years.

Tragic Shipwreck

Their parents die in a storm at sea. Elsa becomes heir to the throne, and Aerindelle prepares for her coronation.

Coronation Day

The gates open for the coronation. Elsa is crowned queen. Anna meets Prince Hans, and they quickly fall in love.
