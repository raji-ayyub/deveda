Rainbow Ice Cream

Ingredients

2 cups heavy cream, 1 cup sweetened condensed milk, food coloring (red, blue, yellow, green), sprinkles.

Preparation Guide

Whip the heavy cream until fluffy, then fold in the condensed milk gently. Divide the mixture into four bowls and add different food colors. Layer each color into a container and freeze for 4 to 6 hours. Scoop and add sprinkles before serving

Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients

1 cup flour, ½ cup cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon baking powder, ½ cup sugar, ½ cup butter, 2 eggs, ½ cup milk.

Preparation Guide

Mix the flour, cocoa, and baking powder together. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar. Add eggs and milk, then mix well. Combine the dry and wet ingredients. Pour into cupcake liners and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool and decorate with frosting.

Mini Pizza

Ingredients

ready-made pizza dough or bread slices, ½ cup tomato sauce, 1 cup shredded cheese, toppings like mushrooms, peppers, sweet corn, or pepperoni.

Preparation Guide

Roll out the dough or place bread slices on a tray. Spread tomato sauce on top, then add cheese and your chosen toppings. Bake at 200°C (390°F) for 10 to 12 minutes and serve warm.

Funfetti Cookies

Ingredients

1 cup flour, ½ cup butter, ½ cup sugar, 1 egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, ½ cup rainbow sprinkles.

Preparation Guide

Cream the butter and sugar together, then add the egg and vanilla. Stir in the flour to make dough and fold in the sprinkles. Scoop dough onto a baking tray and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool before serving.
